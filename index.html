About Me

Hello! My name is Karime Saad and I am a Senior at the University of Texas at Austin studying Electrical and Computer Engineering with my technical core focusing in Software Engineering. I am anticipating to graduate in December 2017, and am open to full-time positions in Software Engineering and Front End Web Development.

For the past few years at college I have been discovering what I like doing and I am passionate about. I realized that programming is something I greatly enjoy because it gives me the chance to be as creative as I want to be. I have had the chance to learn programming from very low-level programming languages like C, and C++, to high-level programming languages like Java, Python, and Javascript. Last summer I interned with Target as a Software Engineer where I worked at the Point-Of-Sale department and mainly used C++. At school, I have taken some Java programming classes where I have developed a great understanding of object-oriented programming, and algorithms. Outside of school, I have used my free time to learn web-development and have helped create websites for different organizations by using Javascript, HTML, CSS and Bootstrap.

Although I love programming in general, my main passion is front-end web development. I consider myself very artistic and creative, and have always been interested in art and design. I have experience in Adobe Creative Cloud programs and even took an intensive art and design program at Savannah College of Art and Design. These skills are needed for web developing because they help make the website user-friendly, and pleasing to the user's eye.
